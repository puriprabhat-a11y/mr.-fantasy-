## Content Strategy: Long-Form SEO Authority
Goal: Execute 3,000-word, high-conversion, SEO-optimized travel blogs for Fantasy World Holidays.

### Daily Content Workflow (The "Content Sprint"):
1. /office-hours: Define the search intent. Is this a "best of" list, a detailed guide, or an itinerary?
2. /plan-ceo-review: Challenge the angle. Ensure it sounds like a founder’s expert advice, not generic AI slop.
3. /plan-eng-review: (Map the structure) Create a detailed outline with H2/H3 headers, internal linking strategy, and CTA placement.
4. /design-shotgun: Generate the "Hero" imagery and social media cards to accompany the post.
5. /review: Check for "Search Experience" optimization—ensure readability, tone consistency, and high-conversion hooks.
6. /ship: Format for WordPress, perform final SEO check (Rank Math), and publish.

### Writing Standards:
- Authority: Every post must demonstrate deep, local knowledge of destinations (Andaman, Goa, etc.).
- Structure: Use clear, scannable headers. Aim for 3,000 words broken into 10+ sub-sections.
- Performance: Every 500 words must include a "Strategic CTA" driving traffic to your [Fantasy World Holidays](https://www.fantasyworldholidays.com) packages.
