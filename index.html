Google Ads · Meta Ads · CRO

Travel Lead Machine — Paid Ads Restructure & CPL Reduction

Delhi travel brand · ₹15L+ quarterly ad spend · 90-day sprint

Performance
The Challenge

Google Ads campaigns were running broad match with no negative keywords, landing pages were generic homepage routes, and the Meta campaigns were using creatives that hadn't been updated in 6 months. CPL was high and ROAS was under 2×. Inquiry quality was poor — lots of low-budget, non-serious leads.

Strategy & Actions

Restructured Google campaigns into tightly themed ad groups by package type. Built dedicated landing pages per package with trust signals, urgency elements, and simplified forms. On Meta, created a 3-tier funnel with awareness video ads, retargeting carousels, and WhatsApp CTA conversion ads. Added conversion tracking via GTM for every form submission and WhatsApp click.

Key Wins

Within 90 days, Google Ads ROAS climbed to 4.2×. Meta CPL dropped from ₹420 to ₹244. Lead quality improved significantly — form submissions with travel dates and budget details increased by 55%. Landing page conversion rate went from 2.1% to 2.9%.

Technical SEO · WordPress · CRO

Technical Audit & WordPress Fix — Site Speed, Schema & Conversion Recovery

Travel website · Dual PHP/WordPress architecture · 6-week sprint

Technical
The Challenge

The website had a complex dual PHP/WordPress architecture causing crawl issues, a broken lazy-loading script that was hiding images from Google, a JavaScript counter stuck at zero on the homepage, a footer typo on 40+ pages, and zero schema markup across the site. Bounce rates were high and Core Web Vitals scores were in the red.

Actions Taken

Conducted a full technical audit using Screaming Frog and GA4. Fixed lazy-loading via plugin swap, resolved the JS counter, corrected footer text across all pages with a template fix, implemented TourPackage and LocalBusiness schema, optimised image compression, and added preloading for critical fonts. Also redirected 28 dead internal links.

Outcome

Core Web Vitals passed on mobile and desktop within 3 weeks. Organic click-through rate improved as rich results (star ratings, FAQ snippets) started appearing. Crawl errors dropped from 140+ to under 10. Pages previously not indexed started appearing in Search Console within 5 weeks.

Content Marketing · SEO · Travel Niche

Pillar Content System — High-Intent Travel Keywords at Scale

India tour packages · Honeymoon & family travel segment · Ongoing

Content
The Challenge

The client was competing for high-value keywords like "Kashmir honeymoon package," "Himachal family tour from Delhi," and "Rajasthan tour package" but had no structured content for them. Thin pages, no FAQ sections, no schema, and no internal linking between related packages.

Strategy & Actions

Built a pillar + cluster content architecture targeting honeymoon, family, and adventure segments. Each pillar had dedicated destination pages, a comparison hub, and 4–6 supporting blog posts per destination. Added itinerary tables, hotel recommendation sections, FAQ schema, and cross-linking between packages. Keyword density kept natural, with semantic variations throughout.

Outcome

Pillar pages began ranking on Page 1 for 12 high-intent keywords within 5 months. "Honeymoon packages from Delhi" category moved from position 28 to position 6. Blog content drove 1,800+ monthly informational visits with 4.2% conversion to package inquiry pages.
