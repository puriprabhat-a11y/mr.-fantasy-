Welcome to My Digital Marketing Blog

Welcome to my blog. I’m Prabhat Puri, a Delhi-based digital marketer with practical experience in SEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads, WordPress, CRO, analytics, and content strategy.

My core expertise is travel marketing, where I work on improving visibility, leads, and conversions for India-focused travel businesses. Through this blog, I’ll share insights, strategies, lessons, and practical ideas from my work in digital growth.

What You’ll Find Here

  • SEO tips for websites and landing pages.
  • Google Ads and Meta Ads strategies for lead generation.
  • Travel marketing ideas for tour and holiday businesses.
  • WordPress, content, and conversion optimization tips.
  • Real-world learnings from campaigns, websites, and client projects.

Why I Started This Blog

I created this blog to document my experience, share useful marketing knowledge, and build a space where I can publish ideas that help businesses grow online. I also want this page to reflect my professional journey, skills, and practical approach to marketing.

My Focus Areas

My main focus is on SEO growth, paid advertising, technical website improvements, and performance marketing for service-based businesses, especially in the travel sector.

Final Thoughts

This is the beginning of my blog journey. I’ll keep sharing useful articles on SEO, ads, websites, and travel marketing with a practical and results-focused approach.
